Numerical Analysis of Partial Differential Equations

MATH576A

  • When: Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 1pm to 1.50pm
  • Where: MATH building, room 501.

Material covered

  • Chapter 0. Preliminaries.
  • Chapter 1. Ordinary differential equations.
  • Chapter 2. Fourier analysis.
  • Chapter 3. Finite difference approximations.
  • Chapter 4. Accuracy, stability and convergence.
  • Chapter 5. Dissipation, dispersion, and group velocity.
  • Chapter 6. Spectral methods.
